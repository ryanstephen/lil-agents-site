lil agents

Privacy Policy

March 22, 2026

lil agents was built with privacy in mind. It runs entirely on your Mac, doesn't require an account, and sends no personal data anywhere.

Your data stays on your Mac

lil agents is a native macOS app that displays animated characters on your dock. It plays custom animations bundled with the app and calculates your dock size to position the characters. No project data, file paths, or personal information is collected or transmitted.

Claude AI

When you click a character, lil agents opens a terminal that runs Claude Code CLI locally. Your conversations are handled entirely by the CLI process. lil agents does not intercept, store, or transmit your chat content. Any data sent to Anthropic is governed by their terms and privacy policy.

No accounts

No account, no login, no user database, no analytics.

Changes

If this policy changes, the date above will be updated.
